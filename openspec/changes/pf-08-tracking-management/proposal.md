## Why

The PocketFlow-Rust porting project involves multiple OpenSpec changes, complex dependencies, and stakeholder coordination. A robust tracking management system is needed to monitor progress, manage risks, track milestones, and communicate with stakeholders. This system will integrate with OpenSpec changes to provide real-time visibility and automated reporting.

## What Changes

- Create a comprehensive tracking management plan for the PocketFlow-Rust porting project
- Define progress tracking across all OpenSpec changes with automated aggregation
- Specify risk management framework with identification, assessment, and mitigation
- Establish milestone tracking with clear criteria and dependencies
- Provide stakeholder reporting with customizable dashboards and notifications
- Integrate with OpenSpec lifecycle for automated progress detection

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `tracking-management`: Plan for tracking management system covering progress, risks, milestones, and stakeholder reporting for the PocketFlow-Rust porting project

### Modified Capabilities
- None (this is a new tracking management plan)

## Impact

- Creates a new OpenSpec change that guides the implementation of tracking management
- Defines the architecture for project visibility and control
- Establishes risk management and mitigation processes
- Provides foundation for stakeholder communication and reporting
- Enables data-driven decision making for the porting project
